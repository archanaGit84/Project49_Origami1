Did you know???

  1. There are two types of camels: One humped or “dromedary” camels and two humped Bactrian camels.
  2. Camels have three sets of eyelids and two rows of eyelashes to keep sand out of their eyes.
  3. Camels can completely shut their nostrils during sandstorms
  4. Camels can travel at up to 40 miles per hour – the same as a racehorse!
  5. Don’t make a camel angry – they can spit as a way to distract whatever they think is a threat
  6. Their humps let them store up to 80 pounds of fat which they can live off for weeks and even months!

Ready for some interesting facts?

  • Chameleons don't change color due to their surroundings. They change it as per their mood
  • Two thirds of chameleon species live in Madagascar
  • They move around with the help of their tails and toes
  • World’s tiniest chameleon, Brookesia micra can fit on the tip of a match.
  • A chameleon’s tongue is about twice as long as its body.

Some facts about Panda

  • Pandas are born pink. They become black and white after three weeks
  • All a panda does all day is eat and sleep
  • Panda can eat 12-38 kgs of bamboo per day
  • Pandas have 6 toes to grasp bamboo
  • Strange behaviour! Pandas love to eat bamboo
